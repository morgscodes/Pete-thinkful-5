About

Hi! I'm Pete Thinkful

I'm an artist living in Denver, Colorado

As an artist, I'm interested in:

Please feel free to take a look at my website and feel free to contact me.

Pete's Background

After graduating college, I became an art teacher working with beginners and professionals. I love art and my works have been featured in major art galleries around the world.

If you're looking for an artist or if you're an artist looking for a collaborator for your next project, please reach out! I'm so excited to work with other artists to create art.
